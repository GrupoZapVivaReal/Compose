TwoStepDisplayUnit

public protocol TwoStepDisplayUnit

Most people configure and define a cell inside the cellForItem/cellForRow method of the dataSource. But you can get some performance boost if you define the state of your cell in the WillDisplayItem/Row method instead This protocol represents an unit that will do the cell setup in two steps. You still can configure your cell in configure:(view:). But you can also define it’s state inside beforeDisplay(view:)

  • beforeDisplay(view:)

    Entry point for the willDisplayCell that the collectionView/tableView delegate will call. You can define the state for your view here.
